# Privacy Policy for Little Moon

**Last Updated: February 7, 2026**

## English

### Data Collection
Little Moon does **not collect, store, or share any personal information**. 

### What Little Moon Does:
- Plays soothing sounds (white noise, rain, ocean waves) locally on your device
- Stores your sound preferences and settings locally on your device only
- Does not require an account or login
- Does not connect to the internet
- Does not use analytics or tracking tools
- Does not share any data with third parties

### Local Storage
The app stores the following data locally on your device:
- Your last selected sound
- Volume settings
- Timer preferences
- Liked sounds

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

### Third-Party Services
Little Moon does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

### Children's Privacy
Little Moon is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

### Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

### Contact Us
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:
- Email: shenxianpeng@gmail.com
- GitHub: https://github.com/shenxianpeng/Little-Moon

## 中文

### 数据收集
Little Moon **不收集、存储或分享任何个人信息**。

### Little Moon 的功能：
- 在您的设备上本地播放舒缓的声音（白噪音、雨声、海浪声）
- 仅在您的设备上本地存储您的声音偏好和设置
- 不需要账户或登录
- 不连接互联网
- 不使用分析或跟踪工具
- 不与第三方分享任何数据

### 本地存储
应用在您的设备上本地存储以下数据：
- 您最后选择的声音
- 音量设置
- 定时器偏好
- 喜欢的声音

这些数据从不离开您的设备，我们或任何第三方都无法访问。

### 第三方服务
Little Moon 不使用任何第三方服务、分析工具或广告网络。

### 儿童隐私
Little Moon 对所有年龄段的用户都是安全的。我们不会有意收集任何人（包括儿童）的信息。
